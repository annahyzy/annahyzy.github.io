ANNA HYZY

Journalist, designer, Hoosier

Hello!

I'm Anna Hyzy and this is my portfolio website for my design and reporting.

I'm a member of the Indiana University class of 2017, majoring in journalism with a second concentration environmental management. My j-school specialization is journalism design and graphics.

I've been very lucky to intern at some of the best papers in visual journalism, spending two summers at the Buffalo News and one at the Star Tribune.

On campus, I'm involved in our completely student-run newspaper, the Indiana Daily Student (to the extent that I'm virtually always in the newsroom). On the IDS staff, I've been a page designer, beat reporter, design chief and a desk editor on the campus desk. I've also been editor in chief and art director (simultaneously) of our magazine, Inside.

Last fall, I worked as the managing editor of presentation at the IDS where I redesigned our inside page section flags and updated our style guide.

Right now, I'm working on an in-depth digital project on abortion restrictions in the state of Indiana and their effects on women in the state.

I'm passionate about news design and its power to tell a story, draw readers in and bring words to life. While I would call myself a designer, I'd also like to think I'm a storyteller above all-else, and I'm always looking for exciting new ways to create visual stories.

When I'm not working, you're likely to find me petting the nearest dog, lettering, hiking or doing yoga.

If you have any questions or you'd just like to chat, please reach out. I can be reached at akhyzy@indiana.edu or on Twitter @annakhyzy

Environmental skillsets

Toxicity assessment

Capable of assessing toxicity levels of a pollutant based on dose, response and exposure. Thus am able to recognize the severity of a chemical exposure accurately.

Working knowledge of environmental law and regulation

After extensive coursework, I have a working knowledge of all major laws governing air quality, water quality and waste management in the United States. I also have an understanding of the permitting and monitoring program that regulators are responsible for.

Scientific communication

I pride myself on my ability to explain complicated scientific matters in a way any non-scientist could understand. I think a lot of the problems we encounter in terms of environmental action are based in a findamental lack of understanding so this issue is particularly close to my heart.

Hazardous waste management

I have an understanding of how hazardous waste disasters are handled both propperly and improperly, what communications are legally required and what actions are legally required.

Risk assessment and management

Based on a number of factors, I am able to assess the level of risk and thus the level of safety of environmental-toxin situations. I also have knowledge of particular remediation methods possible to mitigate risk.
